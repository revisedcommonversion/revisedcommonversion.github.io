Psalm 93

The majesty, stability, power, and holiness of Christ's kingdom, 1-5.

1 The LORD reigns. He is clothed with majesty. The LORD is clothed with strength, with which he has girded himself. The world is also established so that it cannot be moved.

2 Your throne is established of old. You are from everlasting.

3 The floods have lifted up, O LORD, the floods have lifted up their voice. The floods lift their waves.

4 The LORD on high is mightier than the noise of many waters, even mightier than the mighty waves of the sea.

5 Your testimonies are very sure. Holiness is suitable for your house, O LORD, forever.
