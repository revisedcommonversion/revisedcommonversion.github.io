Harmony of the Gospels

The bellow reading plan is taken from John Calvin's Commentary on the Bible. It is the order of the Scriptures given in the section called Harmony of the Evangelists (or Harmony of the Gospels). This reading plan only covers Matthew, Mark, and Luke. Due to the length of the list, I have split the plan up across three parts in the tables below. The rows in the tables separate each day's worth of Scriptures to read.
