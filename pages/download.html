Known Issues

Amazon Kindle

UPDATE 8-14-2015: The MOBI file available below for download may initially display a generic cover when downloaded to your device, but once you open the book, the proper cover should be displayed. (Thanks to reader Joshua Holman for this update.)

Barnes & Noble Nook

UPDATE 4-5-2015: The words of Christ are able to be displayed in the appropriate blue color on the Nook by enabling the option, "Publisher defaults." For greyscale devices, this option will display the words of Christ in a dark grey color. (Thanks to reader Joshua Holman for this update.)
