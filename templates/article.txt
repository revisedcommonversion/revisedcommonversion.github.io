  <p>Today, a new digital edition of the RCV has been released. This is the r2003 edition. This edition contains minor updates that span the entirety of the text of the RCV in addition to the <a href="rcv-logo-update.html">updated logo</a>.</p>
<p>This edition will include all digital files available on the <a href="../download.html">Download</a> page and the <a href="https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008GWPCGG/">Kindle edition</a> on the Amazon Kindle book store.
